ARF: New Website Launched

The Asian Racing Federation (ARF) is delighted to announce the launch of its new Website. Featuring a modern, user-friendly design and an extensive catalogue of ARF work and resources, the new site will serve as the digital home for the ARF, facilitating engagement with members, the wider racing and sports industries and key stakeholders.

We cordially invite you to visit the website to learn more about us.

ARF Members

The ARF comprises 29 national racing authorities and racing-related organisations from across Asia, Oceania, Africa and the Middle East.

Publications
ARF Economic & Social Impact Study

Thoroughbred Racing in ARF Jurisdictions: An Economic and Social Impact Assessment

Thoroughbred Racing in ARF Jurisdictions: An Economic and Social Impact Assessment quantifies the economic and social impact of the thoroughbred racing and breeding industries on ARF member jurisdictions across the 2018/19 racing seasons. The report paints a compelling picture of an industry that contributes positively to society.
ARF Council on Anti-illegal Betting and Related Financial Crime

Good Practices in Addressing Illegal Betting

Released in October 2020, Good Practices in Addressing Illegal Betting highlights the threats posed by illegal betting and related corruption to the integrity of horse racing and other sports. The landmark work engages eminent industry experts to provide practical guidance for administrators and key stakeholders to mitigate against and combat illegal betting-related corruption.

Webinar

“Racing Through the Global COVID-19 Pandemic”

The ARF organised a series of webinars in 2020 titled Racing Through the Global COVID-19 Pandemic. With participation from industry leaders from racing, wagering, marketing, and anti-corruption, the series covered many of the challenges and opportunities facing racing brought by the pandemic. The ARF Council on Anti-Illegal Betting and Related Financial Crime also released a launch of their Handbook for horseracing and other sports to uphold integrity in 2021.

Replay of the webinars are now accessible on the Website.

Asian Racing Conference

The ARC is a major event on the international racing calendar. Attracting and engaging some of the foremost leaders from across racing and sports, the ARC shapes global and regional agendas to continuously advance the racing industry. Stay up to date on information about the 39th ARC and watch replays of previous ARC videos on the website.
